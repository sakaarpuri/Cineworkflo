# CineWorkFlo Style Preset Thumbnails — Image Generation Prompts

## Technical Specs
- Format: WebP
- Size: 320 x 180
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- File size target: 25–60 KB (hard max 80 KB)
- No text burned into image
- One image per preset

## Visual Principles
- Mood thumbnail, not a literal scene
- Strong color/lens/light signature readable at small size
- Simple composition with bold shapes and color blocks
- Avoid tiny faces, fine detail, small text, or complex scenes

### 1. desert-minimalism.webp

A vast amber desert landscape with a single tiny silhouette figure on a dune ridge against a hazy ochre sky. Single hard directional light source from the left casting a long shadow. Volumetric dust haze in the air catching light rays. Desaturated warm amber tone dominating the entire frame. Massive negative space — 80% empty sky and sand. 16:9, ultrawide cinematic framing, photorealistic.

### 2. humid-neon-noir.webp

Hyper-saturated colors smearing across wet surface. Heavy film grain texture. No readable text on signs — just abstract neon color shapes. Claustrophobic tight framing, 16:9, photorealistic.

Dreamy, luminous, 16:9, photorealistic.
