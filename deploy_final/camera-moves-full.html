CAMERA MOVES

Classic Camera Movements

The foundational moves used in every film, TV show, and video.
Hover each card to see how it works.

CLASSIC MOVE
Dollypush in / pull back
SIDE VIEW
HOVER TO PLAY

The camera physically moves forward or backward on a track. Unlike zoom, perspective genuinely shifts — foreground and background move at different rates.

🎬Feels like: Walking into the scene. Builds intimacy, tension, or a slow reveal.
✏️ ADD TO YOUR PROMPT
slow dolly in toward subject, camera pushes forward on a track, cinematic push into close-up
CLASSIC MOVE
Panrotate left / right
TOP-DOWN VIEW
HOVER TO PLAY

The camera rotates left or right on a fixed axis — it stays in place, only the direction it points changes. Used to follow action or reveal a wide scene.

🎬Feels like: Turning your head to follow someone walking past. Great for reveals and establishing wide shots.
✏️ ADD TO YOUR PROMPT
slow pan left to right, camera rotates on fixed axis, wide establishing pan across the scene
CLASSIC MOVE
Tiltpivot up / down
SIDE VIEW
HOVER TO PLAY

The camera pivots up or down on a fixed tripod — staying in place while the lens tilts vertically. Classic for scanning a tall subject from feet to face.

🎬Feels like: Looking up at a skyscraper, or slowly revealing a character from shoes to eyes.
✏️ ADD TO YOUR PROMPT
slow tilt up from feet to face, camera pivots upward on fixed tripod, low-to-high vertical reveal
CLASSIC MOVE
Zoomlens in / lens out
SIDE VIEW
HOVER TO PLAY
FIXED

The camera stays completely still — only the lens changes focal length, making the subject appear closer or further. Creates a flatter, more compressed look than a dolly.

🎬Feels like: Looking through binoculars. The subject gets bigger but the depth of the world flattens.
✏️ ADD TO YOUR PROMPT
slow zoom in on subject, camera stays fixed, lens compresses, telephoto zoom push
CLASSIC MOVE
Tracking Shotfollow sideways
TOP-DOWN VIEW
HOVER TO PLAY

Camera and subject move together sideways at the same speed, keeping the subject perfectly framed throughout. The camera never gets closer or further away.

🎬Feels like: Running alongside someone. Keeps energy and the subject always centred in frame.
✏️ ADD TO YOUR PROMPT
tracking shot following subject, camera moves parallel sideways, side-on tracking as character walks
CLASSIC MOVE
Crane / Jibrise up / sweep down
SIDE VIEW
HOVER TO PLAY

The camera is mounted on a long arm and sweeps vertically — rising high above or swooping down to ground level, keeping the subject in frame throughout.

🎬Feels like: A bird taking flight over the scene. Creates grandeur, scale, and epic transitions.
✏️ ADD TO YOUR PROMPT
crane shot rising up from ground level, camera sweeps upward on jib arm, epic rising reveal pulling back and up

DYNAMIC / CINEMATIC
Handheldsmooth / natural
SIDE VIEW
HOVER TO PLAY

A handheld camera has subtle, organic drift from breathing, footsteps, and micro-corrections. It feels intimate and human without looking chaotic.

🎬Feels like: Present and personal. Adds realism, tension, and immediacy.
✏️ ADD TO YOUR PROMPT
handheld camera, smooth natural micro-drift, human-operated, subtle breathing sway
DYNAMIC / CINEMATIC
Steadicamfollow behind
REAR FOLLOW
HOVER TO PLAY

A stabilised rig creates a gliding follow that feels human but controlled. The camera moves with the subject without the jitter of handheld.

🎬Feels like: Floating alongside the action. Smooth, immersive, and cinematic.
✏️ ADD TO YOUR PROMPT
steadicam follow, smooth glide, stable tracking, no shake
DYNAMIC / CINEMATIC
Whip Pansnap rotation
TOP-DOWN VIEW
HOVER TO PLAY

A fast snap pan that creates motion blur. Often used as an energetic transition between moments or locations.

🎬Feels like: Whipping your head to catch something. Punchy, urgent, and stylised.
✏️ ADD TO YOUR PROMPT
whip pan, fast snap rotation, motion blur transition, energetic cut
DYNAMIC / CINEMATIC
Dutch Angletilt axis (roll)
SIDE VIEW
HOVER TO PLAY

The camera is tilted on its roll axis, making the horizon diagonal. A classic way to suggest unease, imbalance, or psychological tension.

🎬Feels like: Something is off. Anxiety, instability, or a world out of balance.
✏️ ADD TO YOUR PROMPT
dutch angle, tilted horizon, camera roll, uneasy tension
