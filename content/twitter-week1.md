## Tweet Thread: "Why Most AI Video Prompts Fail"

1/ AI video tools are incredible.

But 90% of people get mediocre results because they don't understand prompt engineering.

Here's why most prompts fail (and how to fix it):

2/ Problem 1: Vague descriptions
❌ "A beautiful sunset"
✅ "Golden hour sunset over coastal cliffs, dramatic orange and purple gradient sky, silhouetted rocks, cinematic wide shot, 4K"

Specificity = Quality

3/ Problem 2: Wrong aspect ratio
❌ Ignoring --ar parameter
✅ Always specify: --ar 16:9 for landscape, --ar 9:16 for vertical

Match your output to your platform.

4/ Problem 3: Missing motion description
❌ "Person walking"
✅ "Person walking toward camera, steady handheld camera movement, slight bounce, documentary style"

Motion needs verbs.

5/ Problem 4: No style reference
❌ "Corporate video"
✅ "Corporate interview lighting, soft key light from left, neutral background, shallow depth of field, professional broadcast quality"

Reference existing styles.

6/ Problem 5: Not iterating
First prompt rarely works.

Change ONE variable at a time:
• Lighting
• Camera angle
• Motion description
• Subject details
