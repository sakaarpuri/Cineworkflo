# CineWorkflow Marketing Strategy

## 1. Content Marketing (Daily)

### Twitter/X Strategy
- **3 tweets/day** about AI video tips
- **1 thread/week** deep dive on prompt techniques
- Engage with Runway/Pika/AI video community
- Share before/after prompt comparisons

### YouTube/Video Content
- **2 shorts/week** showing prompt results
- **1 tutorial/month** on specific shot types
- Screen recordings of Shot-to-Prompt in action

### Blog/SEO
- Target keywords: "AI video prompts", "Runway prompts", "Pika prompts"
- 2 articles/week on filmmaking + AI
- Guest posts on filmmaker blogs

## 2. Community Marketing (Daily)

### Reddit
- r/filmmakers
- r/VideoEditing
- r/RunwayML
- r/ArtificialIntelligence
- Answer questions, share value, soft pitch

### Discord Communities
- Runway Discord
- AI Video Creators
- Filmmaking servers
- Be helpful first, mention product when relevant

### IndieHackers/Product Hunt
- Build in public
- Share revenue/usage stats
- Launch on Product Hunt when ready

## 3. Influencer Outreach (Weekly)

### Micro-Influencers (1K-10K followers)
- Filmmakers using AI tools
- YouTube tutorial creators
- TikTok video editors
- Offer free Pro access for review/honest feedback

### Target List:
- [ ] Compile 50 AI filmmaker accounts
- [ ] DM with personalized pitch
- [ ] Offer affiliate commission (20%)

## 4. Paid Marketing (Month 2+)

### Google Ads
- Keywords: "Runway prompts", "AI video prompts", "Pika tutorial"
- Budget: $10-20/day to start

### Twitter/X Ads
- Target: Filmmakers, video editors, content creators
- Promote top-performing organic tweets

### Reddit Ads
- r/filmmakers sponsored posts
- Native-looking content

## 5. Product-Led Growth

### Free Tier Strategy
- 25 prompts = enough to show value
- Watermarked outputs
- Upgrade prompt in app

### Referral Program
- Give 10 free prompts for each referral
- Both parties benefit

### Viral Loops
- "Created with CineWorkflow" badge on outputs
- Easy share to social

## 6. Email Marketing

### Sequence for Free Users
- Day 0: Welcome + best prompts
- Day 3: How to use Shot-to-Prompt
- Day 7: Case study/success story
- Day 14: Limited time discount

### Weekly Newsletter
- New prompts added
- AI video news
- Community highlights

## 7. Partnerships

### Tool Integrations
- Partner with Runway (affiliate)
- Pika referral program
- Descript, CapCut communities

### Cross-Promotion
- Other filmmaker tools
- Stock footage sites
- Music licensing platforms

## 8. Metrics to Track

### Leading Indicators
- Website visitors
- Email signups
- Free tier usage
- Social engagement

### Lagging Indicators
- Conversion rate (free → paid)
- Revenue
- Churn (if any - it's lifetime)
- Customer lifetime value
